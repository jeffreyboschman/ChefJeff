## Ingredients
- 2 [[Salmon Fillet]]
- 1 large [[Lemon]], thinly sliced
- Salt
- Pepper
- Dried [[Basil]] & [[Parsley]]
- Olive Oil

---

## Steps
- Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius
- Slice lemons and layer under and over the salmon filet
- Sprinkle salt, pepper, and herbs
- Drizzle olive oil on top
- Bake for 15 minutes
