## Ingredients
- [[Tofu]], cut into ~4 cm square, 1 cm thick pieces
- [[Soy Sauce]]
- [[Oyster Sauce]]
- [[Ginger]], grated
- (optional) [[Green Onion]], chopped

---
## Steps
- Microwave tofu for 1 minute. Drain off excess water
- Add all toppings
- itadakimasu~
