## Ingredients

- 2 [[Avocado]]
- 1/6 [[White Onion]], diced
- 1 [[Tomatoes]] or 6 Baby Tomatoes (vine ripened), diced
- salt and pepper to taste
- half a [[Lime]]
- (optional) [[Jalapeno]]

---
## Steps
- mush up avocado
- add everything 
- stir
