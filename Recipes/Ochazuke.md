## Ingredients
- Rice
- [[Green Tea]] (genmaicha is the best)

**Toppings**
- [[Umeboshi]]
- [[Tsukemono]]
	- ex. fukujinzuke (the red one), 
- [[Shiso Kombu Tsukudani]] (I think)
- (optional) [[Smoked Salmon]] or [[Easy Salmon]], flaked

---
## Steps
- put rice in bowl
	- heat up in microwave if leftover rice
- put toppings in rice bowl
- add tea on top
- itadakimasu~
